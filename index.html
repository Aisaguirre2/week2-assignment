Fruits and Vegetables

There are many varities of fruits and vegetables to choose from, as well as prepare and serve. Fruits and vegetables contain many important vitamins and minerals that can help to keep you healthy. Typically you want to aim for fruits and veggies that are in season, otherwise frozen or canned veggies are just as nutricious.

Fruits and vegetables are low in fat, salt, and sugar. They are a good source of dietary fiber, which can make you feel fuller for longer and prevent overconsumption of food. As part of a well-balanced, healthy diet and an active lifestyle, a high intake of fruit and vegtebales can help you:

  • Reduce obesity and maintain a healthy weight
  • Lower your cholesterol
  • Lower your blood pressure
